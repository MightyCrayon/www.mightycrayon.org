Financial Institutions

NexBank is a trusted partner to institutions nationally, helping them to accelerate growth, maximize earnings, improve efficiencies and expand their market presence. Our professionals hold extensive experience and offer unique expertise that allow our clients to strengthen their financial position and enhance their competitive edge.

  1. Revolving Lines of Credit and Holding Company Term Loans

Title Services

NexBank Title provides a full range of title and closing services for both residential and commercial properties nationwide. We combine individualized service and industry experience to assist in all stages of the title and closing process, and to deliver an improved experience and outcome for every client.

 

As an agent of three nationally recognized title underwriters, Fidelity National, First American Title Insurance Company, and Old Republic National Title Insurance, NexBank Title offers its clients access to a comprehensive list of resources and tools for every type of transaction.

 

  1. Residential Services

    Whether you are buying, selling or refinancing, our professionals work with you to facilitate the process and make the experience seamless. We communicate and coordinate with all parties, identify and reconcile any areas of concern, and deliver an accurate and secure process that protects our clients’ investments.

  2. Lender Services

    Our professionals understand every process is unique to the lender and transaction. We combine the experience and knowledge of the title services and mortgage lending industries to assist lenders and mortgage brokers with their title and closing needs in an expedited manner. NexBank Title focuses on building long-term relationships to help minimize client staffing and increase efficiencies.

  3. Commercial Services

    NexBank Title offers a single point of contact and individualized services to streamline the process. We provide a wide range of capabilities and an experienced team of professionals to deliver timely closings, reduced costs and consistent results.

Client Login

Through Paperless Closer, our online transaction management system, you can manage your purchase, refinance or loan process. Our system provides step-by-step transaction information and allows you to print and download documents, as well as manage the order online, rather than on the phone, through email or fax. Your NexBank Title representative will assist you in setting up a Paperless Closer login.
